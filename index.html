About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio, I am a web developer with 4 years of experience working from simple projects such as a static web, to slightly larger projects such as sales management systems, inventory and others.

My growth in web development began in an undertaking that later became a technology consultancy called Nextscale in which I was able to obtain all the knowledge that I continue to put into practice to date, which were not only the typical skills of a web developer such as for example, javascript or its different frameworks, if not I also gained knowledge in the management of work methodologies such as waterfall and scrum which are one of the most used today by many companies.

To this day I continue in a learning process, but I am fully prepared to face any challenge that may arise and do it in the best possible way.
